Modern American Restaurant and Cocktail Bar

Serving contemporary New England dishes with a commitment to supporting local farms, brewers and distilleries. Bistro 63 regularly features seasonal specials using locally grown produce, an ever changing draft list of local and legendary brews, and seasonal cocktail list! As of 2018 we are proud to feature a 100% organic, biodynamic, and sustainable wine list. With over 250 craft and specialty spirits, we are proud to have some very inspired mad scientists behind the bar to cultivate our world class beverage program. Come in anytime and ask us about having a wine, beer, or spirits tasting!
